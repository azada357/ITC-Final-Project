Program Structure

A 14-day cultural and leadership experience across world-class destinations.

🗓️

Duration

The program lasts 14 days (2 weeks):

  • 2 days → Travel & hotel check-in/check-out
  • 12 days → Activities and experiences

3-Day Experience Cycle

Day 1 – Leadership Conference

Workshops, lectures, and idea-sharing sessions.

Day 2 – Cultural Exchange

Participants present and experience different cultures.

Day 3 – City Exploration

Visits to historical sites and local attractions.
